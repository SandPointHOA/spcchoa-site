## Security Services

The Sand Point CC HOA contracts with **Seattle's Finest Security** to provide nightly guard coverage for the neighborhood and shared club facilities. Two guards are on duty each night:

- **Guard 1:** 7:00 PM – 3:00 AM
- **Guard 2:** 9:00 PM – 5:00 AM

Guards conduct community-wide patrols and maintain a security presence at club access points.

## Reporting a Security Concern

If you observe suspicious activity or a security concern:

1. **For emergencies, always call 911 first.**
2. Call the on-duty guard directly at **425-454-5011**, or email **[SandPoint@seafinest.com](mailto:SandPoint@seafinest.com)**.
3. You may also contact the HOA board member assigned to security, Josh Hritz, at **[joshua.hritz@gmail.com](mailto:joshua.hritz@gmail.com)**.

## Reporting a Crime

Please report any crime — no matter how small — to the Seattle Police Department. Call 911 and note that it is a non-emergency call, or reach the North Precinct at **206-625-5011**. Reporting all incidents, including theft from an unlocked car or home, helps the whole community track patterns and stay alert.

## Solicitors

There are no solicitors allowed in the neighborhood. If a solicitor comes to your home, please let them know. If they refuse to leave or move on to other streets, call 911. You may also notify Property Manager Joe Flick at **206-999-6161**.

## Looking Ahead

The board is actively pursuing a tech-integrated security model and conducting a formal audit of the current security contract. Residents expressed strong interest in enhanced technology, including cameras, in the 2026 security survey. Updates will be shared via the HOA newsletter.
