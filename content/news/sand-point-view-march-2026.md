## What Needs Work?

Every month the board discusses critical issues impacting the physical conditions of our neighborhood. Last year, we made improvements to our roads, sewer lines, and we added some 'good' shade to overly bright streetlights.

This year we're facing challenges with the entrance wall that protects and surrounds the perimeter of our neighborhood. It's cracking and failing in several places, which puts us at risk for a lawsuit if it was to fall. We must make some tough decisions to replace it. As a result, we need to increase our Reserve Budget to help fund it. More info to be shared at the annual meeting.

## Desperate for a Date Night?

One of the best parts of living in Sand Point is our community of families with children of all ages. Many teens are looking to make spending money (and so are their parents!) for spring break and summer.

If your teens would like to babysit, or you need a babysitter, let the HOA know by emailing us at [spcchoa@gmail.com](mailto:spcchoa@gmail.com) and we'll help make the connection. We'll also set up a separate group on WhatsApp for families to connect.
